Who we are?

At GLISCO LTD, we've been delivering high quality construction service in all areas of Rwanda of thousand hills since 2007, later we expanded our services by including materials delivery, with a commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction.
We specialize in:

  • Execution of buildings construction, roads and bridges construction, water supply works and other civil engineering works
  • Carrying out development of master plans of different urban and rural areas, carrying out feasibility studies of buildings, and highways and carrying out feasibility studies of other civil engineering works like valley dams, drinking water supply systems, and environmental infrastructures.
  • Offering services of consultancy in supervision of all civil engineering works.

Our team of experienced professionals combines technical expertise with local knowledge to deliver projects that meet international standards while addressing the specific needs of the Rwandan context.

Projects executed?

GLISCO LTD has successfully completed numerous projects across Rwanda, showcasing our expertise and commitment to quality. Our portfolio includes:

Building construction: We've constructed commercial and residential buildings that stand as landmarks in their communities, featuring modern designs while adhering to strict safety and environmental standards.

Infrastructure development: Our team has engineered and built critical infrastructure including roads, bridges, and water supply systems that serve thousands of Rwandans daily.

Urban planning: We've contributed to the development of master plans for several urban and rural areas, helping shape Rwanda's growing communities.

Each project we undertake reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable development practices that benefit communities across Rwanda.
