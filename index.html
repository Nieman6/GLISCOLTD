Welcome to our company

GLISCO Ltd has been registered and began operations in 2007 as an individual enterprise and opened the doors to become a company limited by shares in 2011 with the mission to offer excellent services, quality and commitment to its clients.

GLISCO Ltd meets the needs of its clients by providing incomparable services, committing to technical excellence and by concluding contracts on due time with high quality products.

GLISCO Ltd is striving to be the best in civil engineering field. To do so GLISCO Ltd will remain competent, responsible, working within budget and schedule.

The satisfaction of GLISCO Ltd's clients is the first pride which strengthens the company.
